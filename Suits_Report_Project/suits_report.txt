# Suits Viewership Report

## 1. Brief Description

*Suits* is an American legal drama television series created by Aaron
Korsh, which premiered on USA Network on June 23, 2011, and concluded on
September 25, 2019. The series is set at a fictional law firm in New
York City. The show follows Mike Ross (Patrick J. Adams), a brilliant
college dropout who starts working as a law associate for Harvey Specter
(Gabriel Macht) despite never attending law school.
